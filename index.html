Privacy Policy – CAS Log Sync

Last updated: 16 December 2025

Data Deletion and Retention

Users have the right to request deletion of their data processed by the CAS Log Sync application.

How users can request data deletion

  • Send an email to: eduard.grigore@nextisolutions.ro
  • Email subject: CAS Log Sync – Data Deletion Request
  • Include company name and CUI for identification purposes

How data can be deleted

  • Users may delete synchronized call data directly from the CAS genesisWorld CRM system
  • Users may delete locally stored application data by clearing app data or uninstalling the application
  • Users may revoke permissions from Android system settings

Data retention

  • The application does not store data on developer-controlled servers
  • Server-side data is managed exclusively by the customer’s CAS genesisWorld CRM system
